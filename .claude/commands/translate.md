# Translate Content

Translate content between Estonian, English, and German.

## Usage
Provide the text and target language. Example:
> /translate [ET→DE] Meie ettevõte pakub IT-tugiteenuseid...

## Supported directions
- ET → EN (Estonian to English)
- ET → DE (Estonian to German)
- EN → ET (English to Estonian)
- DE → ET (German to Estonian)

## Notes
- Preserve formatting (headers, bullet points, bold text)
- Keep technical terms accurate — do not over-translate IT terminology
- For client-facing content, use formal register (Sie in German, Teie in Estonian)
